Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Vibe Check ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device Identifier: A unique anonymous identifier to track your game progress and scores.
  • Game Data: Your answers to daily questions, scores, streaks, and leaderboard rankings.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about how you interact with the app to improve user experience.
  • Push Notification Token: If you enable notifications, we store a token to send you daily reminders.

üîí Important: We do NOT collect your name, email address, phone number, location data, or any other personally identifiable information. All gameplay is completely anonymous.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Track your daily game progress and maintain your streak
  • Calculate and display aggregate voting results
  • Show your ranking on the daily leaderboard
  • Send you daily reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve the app experience through anonymous analytics

Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase services, which employ industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. We retain your game data to maintain your progress and historical statistics.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For data storage, authentication, and analytics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For push notifications
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: For anonymous usage analytics

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable push notifications at any time through your device settings
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Uninstall the app to stop all data collection

Children's Privacy

Vibe Check is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
