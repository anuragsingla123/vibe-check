Frequently Asked Questions

When do new questions drop?
Fresh questions are available every day at midnight UTC. Enable notifications to never miss a drop!
How is my score calculated?
You earn 10 points when your answer matches the majority opinion, and 1 point when you have a unique opinion. Your grade is based on how many times you matched the majority.
What happens if I miss a day?
Missing a day will reset your streak back to zero. Enable daily notifications to help maintain your streak!
Can I play more than once a day?
You can only answer the daily questions once. After playing, you can view the results and leaderboard until the next day's drop.
How do I get on the leaderboard?
The leaderboard shows the top 10 players each day based on their score. Match more majority opinions to score higher and climb the ranks!
Is my data private?
Absolutely! All gameplay is anonymous. We don't collect any personally identifiable information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
How do I enable/disable notifications?
You can manage notification permissions in your device's Settings app under App Notifications for Vibe Check.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us at support@bitboxapps.com with details about the bug, including your device model and what happened. Screenshots help too!
